# ☕ Coffee Shop Sales Analysis Dashboard

An end-to-end data analytics project focused on analyzing coffee shop retail sales data to generate actionable business insights.  
The project uses data analysis and visualization techniques to understand customer behavior, sales performance, and product trends.

---

# 📊 Project Overview

The goal of this project is to analyze coffee shop sales data and extract meaningful insights that help improve operational efficiency and sales performance.

Through this analysis, we explore:
- Customer purchasing patterns
- Product popularity
- Sales distribution across stores
- Peak business hours
- Revenue trends

The insights derived from the analysis can help the business optimize inventory, staffing, and marketing strategies.

# 📈 Key Insights from the Analysis

### 1️⃣ Peak Sales Hours
- Most orders occur between **8 AM and 10 AM**
- This indicates strong morning demand for coffee products

### 2️⃣ Product Category Performance
- **Coffee category contributes the largest share of total sales**
- Bakery and tea products also contribute significantly

### 3️⃣ Beverage Size Distribution
- Regular size orders dominate the sales
- Large and Not Defined categories follow closely
- Small-sized beverages have the lowest share

### 4️⃣ Store Location Performance
Three store locations were analyzed:

- Astoria
- Hell's Kitchen
- Lower Manhattan

All locations show strong sales, but **Hell's Kitchen performs slightly better in total revenue**.

### 5️⃣ Best-Selling Products
Top-performing products include:

- Barista Espresso
- Brewed Chai Tea
- Hot Chocolate
- Gourmet Brewed Coffee
- Brewed Black Tea

These products generate the highest revenue and customer demand.

### 6️⃣ Weekly Sales Trends
Sales remain **fairly consistent across weekdays**, with slight peaks during mid-week and weekends.
